Momentum: A Live Feedback Tool

This tool is used to provide real-time insignt into meetings and presentations through a simple up/down voting mechanism. First, participants and a discussion are defined in the administration panel of the tool. Then, while listening to a presentation or discussion, participants vote as often as they like to indicate their feelings toward the current content. Depending on the use case, votes may indicate like/dislike, agree/disagree, or important/not important. The collected data is saved in a database and used to create various visualizations which provide a number of valuable insights.
